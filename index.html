About Sheepchick Kebab

Founded in 2020, Sheepchick Kebab brings together the best of Persian and Middle Eastern cuisine. Our unique name represents our specialty in both lamb and chicken kebabs, prepared with traditional recipes passed down through generations.

We source only the finest local ingredients to ensure authentic flavors and support local farmers. Every dish is prepared fresh daily in our kitchen.
